Delivery & Defense

Project validation, strategic justification, and lessons learned.

1. Technical Feasibility Validation

Our Proof of Concept (PoC) successfully demonstrated the core value proposition:

  • Sensor Integration: The Compute Module 5 (CM5) + IMX219 setup successfully captured clear images inside a test fridge environment (4°C).
  • AI Performance: The YOLOv8 model achieved an 88% mAP on our custom dataset. Edge inference on RPi5 is stable at < 200ms latency.
  • Connectivity: Real-time inventory updates were pushed to the mobile app via MQTT.

2. Budget Strategy Justification

We prioritized CAPEX on Tooling (€98.5k) early to lock mold lead times for the enclosure. Soft-steel molds allow us to produce ISO-standard units for certification.

OPEX Discipline: We adjusted our BOM estimates to reflect 2025 supply chain realities (CM5 price increase to €65). We balance this by keeping the core team lean (7 people) and using targeted contractors for specific tasks like EMC Pre-scans.

3. Mitigating Market-Related Risks

Addressing adoption, pricing, and competition as per Project Delivery requirements.

Risk: High Entry Price

Competitors like Samsung have economies of scale we lack. A $2,500 price point limits mass adoption.

Strategy: Pivot to "Hardware-as-a-Service". Sell hardware at near-cost ($2000) and monetize via a $5/mo "Zero-Waste" app subscription that guarantees food savings > cost.

Risk: User Churn (Novelty wears off)

Users stop checking the app after 2 months.

Strategy: "Push" over "Pull". The fridge doesn't wait for you to check it; it proactively adds expiring milk to your online grocery cart (integration with Amazon Fresh/Carrefour).

4. Pitfalls & Unforeseen Events

Pitfall: Sensor Fogging

Issue: Condensation blinded the AI during door-open events.

Resolution: Added heating element ring (automotive grade) to lens assembly.

Pitfall: CM5 Shortage

Issue: Raspberry Pi CM5 modules faced 12-week lead times.

Resolution: Activated contingency budget to source from secondary market at +20% premium to maintain schedule.
