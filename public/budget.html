Why this budget is consistent with the project

The total budget of €406,180 is computed from the detailed resources of the SmartCool Pro industrialization phase and is aligned with our Gantt planning and team structure.

  • CAPEX (€98,500): one-time investments (molds, certifications, test bench) enabling large-scale production and lowering the unit cost of each fridge.
  • Staffing (€295,680): 7 key profiles (PM, hardware, cloud, data, embedded, mechanics, purchasing) over 6 months, with realistic gross salaries and employer charges.
  • Operating (€12,000): office rent and software licenses necessary to run the team during the prototype and industrialization phases.

These amounts are consistent with the work breakdown structure: each cost line here maps either to a resource visible in the Team page (skills & personas) or to a task or milestone in the Planning (Gantt). CAPEX items are linked to industrialization gates, while OPEX follows the 6‑month project duration.
